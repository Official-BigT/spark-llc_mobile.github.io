# Privacy Policy for Spark

At Spark we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how Spark collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use the Spark mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Personal Information

Spark may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as:

- **Name** (first and last)
- **Email address** (for login and account recovery)
- **Job title**
- **Bio** (optional profile description)
- **Profile picture** (optional; uploaded by you)
- **LinkedIn** and other social links (optional)
- **Country**
- **Industry and interests** (for matching and networking)
- **Company information** (for exhibitors/partners): company name, logo, sector, country, description, website, offers, positions, and recruiting status
- **Account-related information** (passwords are not stored; we use secure token-based authentication)

### b. Camera and Photo Library Access

Spark requests access to your device's **camera** and **photo library** (gallery) when required for in-app features such as:

- Uploading profile pictures
- Uploading company logos (for exhibitors/partners)
- Scanning QR codes (for event check-in and networking)

We do not access your camera or photos without your explicit action or permission.

### c. Push Notifications

To send you event-related notifications (meeting requests, connection requests, event updates), Spark uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM). We collect and store a **push notification registration ID** (device token) on our servers. You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.

### d. Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data to help improve app performance and user experience, such as:

- App interactions
- Crash reports
- Device type and operating system version

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Spark uses collected information to:

- Provide and improve app functionality
- Enable features that require camera, photo library, or push notification access
- Match you with other attendees, exhibitors, or partners at events
- Display your profile and company information in event directories and networking features
- Facilitate meeting requests and connections between users
- Fix bugs and improve performance
- Communicate important updates (e.g., meeting requests, connection requests)
- Comply with legal obligations

Spark does not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information except:

- **With other event attendees**: Your profile and company information may be visible to other users of the app at the same event, for purposes such as networking, meeting requests, and exhibitor/partner directories.
- **When required by law**: To comply with legal obligations or court orders.
- **With trusted service providers**: For app functionality only, including:
  - Our backend API (hosted infrastructure)
  - Firebase (push notifications)
  - Expo (app updates and build infrastructure)
  - Analytics or crash reporting services (if used)

All third-party services used comply with applicable data protection regulations.

---

## 4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against:

- Unauthorized access
- Loss
- Misuse

However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 5. Your Choices and Rights

You can:

- Deny or revoke camera and photo library permissions at any time via your device settings
- Disable push notifications in your device settings
- Request deletion of your data by contacting us at **support@sparkapp.dev**
- Stop using the app if you disagree with this policy

To request deletion of your data, email us at **support@sparkapp.dev**. We will process your request within 30 days where technically and legally feasible.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Spark is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
